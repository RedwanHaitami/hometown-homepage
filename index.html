Visit Fes

Fes is the oldest and the second largest city of Morocco and its cultural and spiritual capital city; it was founded in 789 under Idrisid rule.

Some of the historic landmarks in Fes

Madrasa Al-Attarine

Madrasa is the Arabic word for any type of educational institution, it is richly-decorated monument built in 1323.

Al-Qarawiyin Mosque-University

It is the oldest university in the world built in 859 and one of the most important religious buildings of Morocco.

Boujeloud Gate

An ornate city gate, built by the French colonial administration in 1913 to serve it as the grand entrance to the old city.

Top three activities to do in Fes

Do artisanal shopping

There are several bazars in old medina where you can find various handcrafted items to buy as souvenirs like pottery, textiles and leather goods.

Take a walk in Jnan Sbil park

The park is near the main medina entrance, full of various gardens, small lake and large trees giving shade to relax away from the hustle and bustle of the medina.

Take food tour

Fes Riads suggests to you a variety of traditional Fassi dishes to taste, known by the harmony of spices and herbs used in every dish.
